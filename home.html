About company

Individual binocular magnifiers Q-Optics TTL system ("Through the lens")

— are made with perfect accuracy for the features of the face of a particular user. Our patented True-Fit ™ digital metering system takes personalization and adjustment of magnifiers to the next level.

Digital measurements make traditional measurements more accurate and add entirely new parameters for optimal magnifier placement.

Working distances are set with centimeter precision, so that each magnifier corresponds to the user's normal working position, without forcing him to adjust to the focal length outside the usual working distance. All workers distances (25-66 cm) are measured individually for each person.

Built in titanium frames, Q-Optics loupes will last you for years.
